About Us

Triplecorn is a property management software as a service application that provides homeowners with the simplest method to manage their rental properties. It is a full property manager that needs no external oversight, as our software and services will handle all tasks and issues. Our company is currently backed by SkyDeck, a Berkeley startup accelerator.

Web & Mobile Specialties

Our application is available as both a webapp and mobile app, so you can access your data at home or on the go.

Our Services

01.

Entry

We conduct screening for all prospective tenants and houses, and we provide a service that generates optimal matchings between them.

  • Online Screening
  • Efficient Service

Management

  • Handyman Service
